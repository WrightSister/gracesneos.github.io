Instructions:
  • Choose your template from the dropdown. (All Bydz formats are relatively the same, just with rules filled in for the desired PAH type.)
  • Copy and paste the PAH lister's listing into the box on the left.
  • Press the "Preview Listing" button.
  • Review the listing that pops up below the textbox. If there are any errors you would like to correct, do so.
  • When satisfied, press the "Add Listing" button.
  • Repeat for all of your listings. Do not refresh or you will lose your progress.
  • When done, press the "Generate Petpage" button.
  • Copy and paste the resulting code into your (empty!) petpage. Be advised that this program generates the entire petpage. You do not need to use a premade code. Credits:
    • Standard format
    • Bydz format + custom images
  • If you want to add more pets to the listing, just keep adding them to the textarea and regenerate the petpage as needed.
  • Before publishing your petpage, double check that you have your rules and other info.
  • That's it, you're done! Go forth and host. The Pound Chat loves you.
Notes:
  • Just copy and paste the user's listing. Do not include portions of the user's signature, or the program will probably fail.
  • List a maximum of two pets per user at a time. Do not lump together multiple users' entries.
  • The "Listing" or "Listing:" line is optional. You can leave it in or just not copy/paste it into the textbox. Should be fine either way.
  • The program can handle notes that users put in their listing so long as those notes are in parentheses.
    • Yes: "Petname - Color Species (800 HSD)"
    • No: "Petname - Color Species 800 HSD"
    You may have to manually correct some entries to fit this convention.
  • Likewise, this app won't correct any misspellings, lowercase entries, or other errors on its own. As long as PAH listers stick to a conventional style like the example in the textbox, there shouldn't be too many problems. If you want to save yourself a headache, clean up the listing a bit before hitting the "Preview Listing" button.
